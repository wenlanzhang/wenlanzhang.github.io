Wenlan Zhang is a second-year PhD student at the The Bartlett Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA), University College London.

She is currently working on her research project titled "Quantifying Digital Injustice: The Utility of Digital Footprint Data in the Global South", under the supervision of Dr. Chen Zhong, Prof. Mark Pelling, and Dr. Faith Taylor.

Wenlan's research interests lie in sustainable smart cities, particularly in Global South countries, and she aims to pursue a career in this sector in the future. Feel free to contact her to discuss potential projects.
